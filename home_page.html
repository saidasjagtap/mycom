Operating model
Project Automation began as the CEO's vision for a more structured, transparent, and efficient way of running IT Operations. The VP designed a custom Databricks tool to identify noise and repeated tickets — bringing visibility to patterns previously lost in day-to-day operations. The Director is executing that vision through daily operating discipline, structured reviews, and stronger process control.
